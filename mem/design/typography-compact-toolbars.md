---
name: Typography & compact toolbars
description: Global typography (Inter Tight + Instrument Serif) and ultra-compact toolbar/header sizing for data-dense pages
type: design
---
- Sans: 'Inter Tight' (UI/body) — geometric, sober, professional
- Serif: 'Instrument Serif' (display/elegant headings) — use `.font-serif`
- Mono: JetBrains Mono (numbers, CIG, IBAN)
- Tracking: body -0.005em, headings -0.022em
- Sticky page headers: px-3 py-1.5 space-y-1.5 (NOT py-3) to maximize table area
- Combobox: default h-8 text-xs (compact)
- DataTable toolbar: search h-7, buttons h-7 px-2, gap-1.5
